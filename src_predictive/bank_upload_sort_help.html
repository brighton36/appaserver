Bank Upload Help

Bank reconciliation is achieved by:

A) Open two Accountancy Model browsers, one on top of the other.

  • In the top browser, log into the Accountancy Model.
  • In the bottom browser, open two tabs. Log into the Accountancy Model in each tab.
  • You should have three Accountancy Model sessions open.
  • In the top browser you will query Bank Upload.
  • In the bottom browser you will:
    • Tab 1: query Journal Ledger.
    • Tab 2: insert missing transactions.

B) In the top browser:

  1. In the top frame: <Load> <Bank Upload>
    • Upload the transaction spreadsheet that you previously downloaded from your bank.
    • It's okay to duplicate transactions as they will be ignored.
    • Be careful not to skip any transactions.
  2. In the top frame: <Lookup> <Ledger> <Bank Upload>
    • Press the <Statistics> radio button.
    • Query where Posted yn = No.
    • Press <Submit>
    • Note the minimum sequence number.
    • Press the <Reset> button.
    • Press the <Sort Order> radio button.
    • Query where Sequence Number ≥ a few less than the minimum sequence number.
      • Warning: only query the Sequence Number. Otherwise, the Bank Running Balance will be off.
    • Press <Submit>
  3. The bottom frame will be titled

    Sort Bank Upload

    • Note the minimum Bank Date.

C) In the bottom browser's first tab:

  1. In the top frame: <Lookup> <Ledger> <Journal Ledger>
    • <Skip> the Prelookup Transaction screen.
    • Query where Transaction Date Time ≥ a few days before the minimum Bank Date and Account = YourCheckingAccount.
    • Press <No Display> for Street Address (maybe), Account, Transaction Count, and Previous Balance.
    • Press <Submit>
  2. The bottom frame will be titled

    Update Journal Ledger

    • Press the <Sort> button over the Transaction Date Time column.

D) Process:

  1. The bank reconciliation process is to synchronize the Bank Running Balance in the top browser with your ledger's Balance in the bottom browser.
  2. Your bank transactions will likely be out of order.
    • In the top browser's bottom frame, press <Move> <Here> to move bank transctions up or down.
      • Notice the Bank Running Balance recalculates.
  3. Your Journal Ledger will likely be missing transactions.
    • In the bottom browser's second tab, insert transactions by either:
      • <Manipulate> <Post Reoccurring Transaction>
      • <Insert> <Ledger> <Transaction>
    • In the bottom browser's first tab, requery by pressing <Submit>.
    • In the bottom browser's bottom frame, reorder by pressing the <Sort> button over the Transaction Date Time column.
  4. Repeat this process until your bank's balance matches YourCheckingAccount balance.
  5. Finally, in the top browser's top frame:
    • Press the <Lookup> radio button.
    • Press <Submit>
    • In the top browser's bottom frame:
      • Set Posted yn = Yes for each row.
