Bank Upload Help

Bank reconciliation is achieved by:

A) Open two PredictiveBooks browsers, one on top of the other.

  • In the top browser, log into PredictiveBooks.
  • In the bottom browser, open two tabs. Log into PredictiveBooks in each tab.
  • You should have three PredictiveBooks sessions open.
  • In the top browser you will query Bank Upload.
  • In the bottom browser you will:
    • Tab 1: query Journal Ledger.
    • Tab 2: insert missing transactions.

B) In the top browser:

  • In the top frame: <Lookup> <Ledger> <Bank Upload>
    • Press the <Statistics> radio button.
    • Press <Submit>
    • Note the maximum bank date.

C) Download your bank transactions:

  1. Open a fresh browser window and log into your bank.
    • Download transactions starting a few days before the maximum date.
    • Save them in either "Excel" or "Comma Separated (csv)" format.
    • It's okay to duplicate transactions as they will be ignored.
    • Be careful not to skip any transactions.
  2. Close the bank browser window when finished.

D) In the top browser:

  1. In the top frame: <Load> <Load Bank Spreadsheet>
    • Upload the transaction spreadsheet that you previously downloaded from your bank.
  2. In the top frame: <Lookup> <Ledger> <Bank Upload>
    • Press the <Statistics> radio button.
    • Query where Bank Running Balance is not empty.
    • Press <Submit>
    • Note the maximum sequence number.
  3. In the top frame:
    • Press the <Reset> button.
    • Query where Sequence Number ≥ the maximum sequence number.
      • Warning: only query the Sequence Number. Otherwise, the Bank Running Balance will be off.
    • Press <Submit>
  4. The bottom frame will be titled

    Update Bank Upload

    • Notice it's already sorted by the Bank Date column.
    • Note the minimum Bank Date.
  5. In the top frame:
    • Press the <Sort Order> radio button.
    • Press <Submit>
  6. The bottom frame will be titled

    Sort Bank Upload

    • Note: you no longer need the top window's top frame. You may want to slide the frame size adjuster up to gain real estate in the bottom frame.

E) In the bottom browser's first tab:

  1. In the top frame: <Lookup> <Ledger> <Journal Ledger>
    • <Skip> the Prelookup Transaction screen.
    • Query where Transaction Date Time ≥ a few days before the minimum Bank Date and Account = YourCheckingAccount.
    • Press <No Display> for:
      • Street Address (maybe)
      • Account
      • Transaction Count
      • Previous Balance
  2. Press <Submit>
  3. The bottom frame will be titled

    Update Journal Ledger

    • Press the <Sort> button over the Transaction Date Time column.
    • Note: you no longer need the bottom window's top frame. You may want to slide the frame size adjuster up to gain real estate in the bottom frame.

F) Process:

  1. The bank reconciliation process is to synchronize the Bank Running Balance in the top browser with your ledger's Balance in the bottom browser.
  2. Your Sort Bank Upload transactions will likely be out of order.
    • In the top browser's bottom frame, press <Move> <Here> to move Sort Bank Upload transctions up or down.
      • Notice the Bank Running Balance recalculates.
  3. Your Update Journal Ledger will likely be missing transactions.
    • In the bottom browser's second tab, insert transactions by either:
      • <Manipulate> <Post Reoccurring Transaction Recent>
      • <Manipulate> <Pay Liabilities>
      • <Shortcut> <Collect Customer Payment>
      • <Insert> <Ledger> <Transaction>
    • When you're finished, requery the bottom browser's first tab.
      • Note: you may need to slide the frame size adjuster down to gain real estate in the top frame.
      • Re-execute paragraph E) steps 2 and 3.
  4. Repeat this process until every Bank Running Balance matches a corresponding Journal Ledger Balance.
  5. <Submit> your changes in the Sort Bank Upload screen.

G) Integrity Checks:

  • Go to the top brower's top frame.
    1. Press the <Lookup> radio button.
    2. Press the <Submit> button.
    3. Go to the top browser's bottom frame.
    4. Press the <Reverse> button over the Sequence Number column.
    5. Notice the first row's Bank Running Balance.
  • Go to the bottom browser's top frame.
    1. <Output> <Trial Balance>
    2. Press the <Submit> button.
    3. Make sure YourCheckingAccount's Debit balance equals the first row's Bank Running Balance.
    4. Make sure Debits = Credits.
    5. <Output> <Balance Sheet> or <Output> <Financial Position>
    6. Press the <Submit> button.
    7. Make sure Assets = Liabilities + Equity
    8. Notice the Investment account balance
  • Go to the top brower's top frame.
    1. <Output> <Account Balance>
    2. Press the <Submit> button.
    3. Make sure the sum of your investments equals your Investment account balance

H) First Time Users:

Manually insert the first row into BANK_UPLOAD, setting the first bank_running_balance.
